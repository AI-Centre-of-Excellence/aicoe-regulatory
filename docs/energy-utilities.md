# Energy & utilities — reliability, grid automation & sector cybersecurity

A curated index of authoritative, primary-source standards and regulation governing electric power systems and utilities: North American reliability and critical-infrastructure protection, international power-utility automation and security standards, and European Union electricity market and grid-cybersecurity law. Every link points to the issuing body's own canonical page.

## Scope & maintenance

- **Primary sources only.** Every link points to the issuing body's own canonical page or legal text.
- **One row per document.** Tables are normalised; no standard is duplicated across sections.
- **Paid standards link to the catalogue.** IEC series (SER) standards are copyrighted; rows link to the official webstore.iec.ch catalogue page.
- **Current versions.** IEC 61850 and IEC 62351 link to the live series (SER) pages, which always resolve to the current published set; NERC standards pages track the enforceable versions on file with FERC.
