# Digital markets & platforms — platform, content-moderation & gatekeeper regulation

A curated index of authoritative, primary-source rules governing online platforms, content moderation, and digital-market gatekeepers in the EU and UK. Every link points to the issuing body's own canonical legal text or official policy page.

## Scope & maintenance

- **Primary sources only.** Every link points to the issuing body's own canonical page or legal text.
- **One row per document.** Tables are normalised; no standard is duplicated across sections.
- **Paid standards link to the catalogue.** ISO/IEC/SAE standards are copyrighted; rows link to the official catalogue page.
- **Current versions.** EU instruments are fully applicable; the UK DMCC Act 2024 regime is being brought into force by phased commencement — update rows as CMA guidance and SMS designations are published.
