## Scope & maintenance

- **Primary sources only.** Every link points to the issuing body's own canonical page or legal text.
- **One row per document.** Tables are normalised; no standard is duplicated across sections.
- **No cross-domain duplication.** The US Children's Online Privacy Protection Rule (COPPA) is indexed under Data protection & privacy and is intentionally not repeated here.
- **Current versions.** The EU CSAM Regulation remains a Commission proposal (COM(2022) 209); rows will be updated to a directive/regulation citation once it is adopted.
