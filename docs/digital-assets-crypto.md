# Digital assets & crypto — regulation, standards & supervisory frameworks

A curated index of authoritative, primary-source regulation and supervisory standards governing crypto-assets, stablecoins and virtual-asset service providers (VASPs). It covers the EU crypto-asset framework, the global anti-money-laundering and prudential standards set by FATF, the Basel Committee and IOSCO, and the leading US federal guidance. Every link points to the issuing body's own canonical page or legal text.

## EU

| Document | Issuer | Link | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|

## Scope & maintenance

- **Primary sources only.** Every link points to the issuing body's own canonical page or legal text, not to secondary summaries or mirrors.
- **One row per document.** Tables are normalised; no standard is duplicated across sections.
- **Verify on change.** When adding or updating a row, confirm the URL resolves to the stated document before committing.
- **Current versions.** MiCA and the recast Transfer of Funds Regulation apply on a phased basis; the FATF targeted updates and IOSCO recommendations are periodically refreshed — update rows as newer editions issue.
