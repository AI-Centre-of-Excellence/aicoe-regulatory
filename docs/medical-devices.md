## Scope & maintenance

- **Primary sources only.** Every link points to the issuing body's own canonical page or document.
- **One row per document.** Keep the tables normalised; do not duplicate a standard across sections.
- **Paid standards.** ISO and IEC standards link to the official catalogue or webstore page; the full text is a paid purchase.
- **Current versions.** FDA QMSR (effective 2 Feb 2026) amends 21 CFR 820 to incorporate ISO 13485. IEC 62304 is under revision. Track new editions and amendments as they publish.
- **Cross-references.** Some entries overlap with the Healthcare topic (SaMD, interoperability) and the AI governance topic (GMLP, AI/ML devices). These entries are the medical-device-specific source.
