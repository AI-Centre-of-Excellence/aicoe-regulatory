## Scope & maintenance

- **Primary sources only.** Every link points to the issuing body's own canonical page or legal text, not to secondary summaries or mirrors.
- **One row per document.** Tables are normalised; no standard is duplicated across sections.
- **Paid standards link to the catalogue.** ISO/SAE standards are copyrighted; rows link to the official catalogue page.
- **Current versions.** ISO 26262:2018, ISO 21448:2022, ISO/SAE 21434:2021 and SAE J3016_202104 are the current editions; UN R155/R156 and (EU) 2019/2144 are updated via UNECE amendments and EU implementing/delegated acts respectively.
