# Telecommunications — electronic communications law & standards

A curated index of authoritative, primary-source telecommunications instruments: European electronic-communications law, United States communications statute and FCC rules, and the international spectrum and network standards issued by the ITU and 3GPP. Every link points to the issuing body's own canonical page or legal text.

## European Union

| Document | Issuer | Link | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| European Electronic Communications Code — Directive (EU) 2018/1972 | EUR-Lex (EU Publications Office) | https://eur-lex.europa.eu/eli/dir/2018/1972/oj/eng | Recasts the EU electronic-communications regulatory framework; Member States applied it from 21 Dec 2020 |
| Open Internet Access ('net neutrality') — Regulation (EU) 2015/2120 | EUR-Lex (EU Publications Office) | https://eur-lex.europa.eu/eli/reg/2015/2120/oj/eng | Enshrines open-internet-access rules and (as amended) intra-EU communications and roaming safeguards |
| BEREC — Body of European Regulators for Electronic Communications | BEREC | https://www.berec.europa.eu/en | EU body coordinating national regulators; issues guidelines (e.g. on net-neutrality implementation) under Regulation (EU) 2018/1971 |

## Scope & maintenance

- **Primary sources only.** Every link points to the issuing body's own canonical page or legal text.
- **One row per document.** Tables are normalised; no instrument is duplicated across sections.
- **International standards link to the issuer's catalogue.** ITU Radio Regulations and ITU-T Recommendations are published (some paid); rows link to the official ITU publication and catalogue pages.
- **Current versions.** India's Telecommunications Act 2023 is indexed under Cybersecurity and is not duplicated here.
