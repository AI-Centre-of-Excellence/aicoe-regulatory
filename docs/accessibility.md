## Scope & maintenance

- **Primary sources only.** Every link points to the issuing body's own canonical page or document, not to secondary summaries or mirrors.
- **One row per document.** Keep the tables normalised; do not duplicate a standard across sections.
- **Verify on change.** When adding or updating a row, confirm the URL resolves to the stated document before committing.
- **Current versions.** WCAG 2.2 is the current W3C Recommendation; WCAG 3.0 is in Working Draft and may change significantly. EN 301 549 v3.2.1 is current but under revision to align with the European Accessibility Act. The ADA Title II compliance dates were extended in 2026; update rows as deadlines shift.
- **Paid standards.** ISO/IEC standards link to the official iso.org catalogue page; the full text is a paid purchase.
