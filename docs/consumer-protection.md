# Consumer protection & advertising — laws, codes & guidance

A curated index of authoritative, primary-source consumer-protection and advertising rules across the European Union, the United States and the United Kingdom. Every link points to the issuing body's own canonical page or legal text: EUR-Lex for EU directives, the Federal Trade Commission and eCFR for US federal rules, the Advertising Standards Authority for UK advertising codes, and legislation.gov.uk for UK statute.

## Scope & maintenance

- **Primary sources only.** Every link points to the issuing body's own canonical page or legal text.
- **One row per document.** Tables are normalised; no instrument is duplicated across sections.
- **Advertising codes link to the administering body.** The CAP and BCAP codes are maintained by CAP/BCAP and enforced by the ASA; rows link to the ASA's official code pages.
- **Current versions.** The Product Liability Directive (EU) 2024/2853 entered into force in 2024 and must be transposed by Member States by 9 December 2026, superseding Directive 85/374/EEC; the Modernisation Directive (EU) 2019/2161 amends rather than replaces the UCPD and Consumer Rights Directive. Rows are updated as transposition and revisions are adopted.
