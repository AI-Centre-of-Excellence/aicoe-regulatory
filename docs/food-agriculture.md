## Scope & maintenance

- **Primary sources only.** Every link points to the issuing body's own canonical page or legal text.
- **One row per document.** Tables are normalised; no standard is duplicated across sections.
- **Paid standards link to the catalogue.** ISO standards are copyrighted; rows link to the official catalogue page.
- **Current versions.** ISO 22000:2018 is the current edition; Codex texts are revised periodically. Track new editions and Codex amendments as they publish.
