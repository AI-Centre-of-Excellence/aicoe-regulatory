# Air quality — ambient & indoor air standards, limit values & regulation

A curated index of authoritative, primary-source guidelines, standards, and regulations governing ambient (outdoor) and indoor air quality. Every link points to the issuing body's own canonical page or document. This covers health-based air-quality guidance, legally binding limit values, and monitoring standards — distinct from the greenhouse-gas and climate-disclosure frameworks catalogued under Sustainability & ESG.

## Scope & maintenance

- **Primary sources only.** Every link points to the issuing body's own canonical page or document, not to secondary summaries or mirrors.
- **One row per document.** Keep the tables normalized; do not duplicate a standard across sections.
- **Ambient vs. indoor.** Sections above separate outdoor (ambient) air-quality law from indoor air standards; greenhouse-gas and climate targets live under Sustainability & ESG, not here.
- **Verify on change.** When adding or updating a row, confirm the URL resolves to the stated document before committing. Some issuers (EUR-Lex, UNECE) block automated fetchers; where a row is flagged, the canonical URL has been confirmed in a browser.
- **Current versions.** Where an instrument has been recast or superseded (for example the EU air quality directives replaced by Directive (EU) 2024/2881), the superseded rows carry the successor in Notes and in the routing metadata.
- **Candidates for expansion.** Non-EU/US/UK jurisdictions (for example India's CPCB National Ambient Air Quality Standards) are pending a confirmed canonical source and are not yet listed.
