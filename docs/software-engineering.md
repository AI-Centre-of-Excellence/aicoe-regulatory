## Scope & maintenance

- **Primary sources only.** Every link points to the issuing body's own canonical page or document.
- **One row per document.** Keep the tables normalised; do not duplicate a standard across sections.
- **Paid standards.** IEEE and ISO/IEC standards link to the official catalogue or webstore page; the full text is a paid purchase.
- **Current versions.** ISO/IEC/IEEE 12207 was updated in 2026; ISO/IEC 25010 was updated in 2023. Track new editions as they publish.
